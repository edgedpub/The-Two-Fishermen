Chapter 03

At this point, the table was not a table anymore but turned into something different: a canvas or a book to tell stories. It was the turn of the Frenchman to ask a question and put the card of the Magician and three cards of Pentacles next to each other. The Italian man meditated on the new card combination and concluded that it was related to his work. So, he took the pile of cards and started selecting new cards.

The first card was the Tower, reading "LA MAISION DIEV", which indicated some type of trouble with the Italian's home or household.

This card was followed by the Magician and the Italian pointed with his finger to himself to state that he was the man on the card and that he had to work to repair the damage to the house.

The meaning of the second card became clear when he pulled the third card, which was the 9 of pentacles. He needed more money and didn't know what to do.

The solution to his problem came with the following chart: the World. The Frenchman associated this card with a trip abroad or to other seas, because only in this way could a fisherman earn more money.

The story took a twist at the end with the Wheel of Fortune. By it, the Italian meant that it had been the right decision to seek work opportunities away from home and that everything had turned out well in the end.
